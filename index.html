Landdd: Art Book Fair

Created the visual identity for Landdd’s 3rd Annual Art Book Fair. The visual identity is referential to the various punctuative marks ubiquitously found in our most beloved publications. Their abstracted forms create an intriguing pattern with dynamism, engaging the audience while feeling visually related to the subject of books. A screen printed tote bag served as the accompanied collateral.
